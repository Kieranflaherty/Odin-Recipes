Fried Eggs

Here is my recipe for fantastic fried eggs! Frying an egg is a valuable life skill. You've come to the right place.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Frying Pan

Steps

  1. Get a frying pan. Put frying pan on hob and turn it on.
  2. Add any oil of your choice to the pan.
  3. Grab some eggs
  4. Break the eggs onto the pan
  5. Let them cook until they start to look done
  6. Turn off the pan and serve the eggs with salt and pepper
